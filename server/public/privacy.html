Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

Last updated: 2026-05-07

1. Information We Collect / 我们收集的信息

1.1 Device Identifier / 设备标识符

We collect a device identifier (IDFV on iOS, Android ID on Android) solely for the purpose of identifying your game records and enabling multiplayer matchmaking. This identifier is not linked to your real-world identity and is not used for tracking across other apps or websites.

我们收集设备标识符（iOS 的 IDFV，Android 的 Android ID），仅用于识别您的游戏战绩和启用人人对战匹配。此标识符不与您的真实身份关联，也不用于跨应用或网站追踪。

1.2 Nickname / 昵称

You may provide a nickname for display in-game and on leaderboards. This is entirely optional and you can change it at any time.

您可以提供昵称用于游戏内和排行榜显示。此项完全可选，您可随时更改。

1.3 Game Statistics / 游戏数据

We record game results (wins, losses, draws) and hero usage statistics to provide leaderboards and personal stats. This data is associated with your device identifier.

我们记录游戏结果（胜/负/平局）和英雄使用统计，以提供排行榜和个人战绩。此数据与您的设备标识符关联。

2. Data We Do NOT Collect / 我们不收集的数据

  • Personal identification (name, email, phone number)
  • 不收集个人身份信息（姓名、邮箱、电话）
  • Location data
  • 不收集位置数据
  • Contacts or social media information
  • 不收集通讯录或社交媒体信息
  • Payment information
  • 不收集支付信息

3. Data Sharing / 数据共享

We do NOT share, sell, or rent your data to any third party. All data is stored on our secure servers and used exclusively for providing the game service.

我们不会与任何第三方分享、出售或出租您的数据。所有数据存储在我们的安全服务器上，仅用于提供游戏服务。

4. Data Security / 数据安全

Game record submissions are protected by HMAC-SHA256 signature verification to prevent forgery. Network communication uses WSS/TLS encryption.

游戏战绩提交受 HMAC-SHA256 签名验证保护以防止伪造。网络通信使用 WSS/TLS 加密。

5. No Tracking / 不追踪

This app does NOT track users across other apps or websites. We do not use any advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs that collect personal data, or cross-app tracking technologies.

本应用不会跨应用或网站追踪用户。我们不使用会收集个人数据的广告 SDK、分析 SDK 或跨应用追踪技术。

6. Children's Privacy / 儿童隐私

This app is suitable for users aged 9 and above. It does not knowingly collect data from children under 13 without parental consent.

本应用适合 9 岁以上用户。我们不会在未经父母同意的情况下故意收集 13 岁以下儿童的数据。

7. Changes / 变更

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page.

我们可能会更新此政策。变更将发布在此页面。

8. Contact / 联系我们

For questions about this policy, contact: GitHub Issues

如有问题，请联系：GitHub Issues
